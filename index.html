About

A more accessible web is a better web for everyone. Platypoda Productions, LLC is the freelancing home of Sylvia Pellicore. I can help you build an accessible website that works well for all your customers. I live and work in Durham, NC, but I'm open to working with you no matter where you are. Whether you need help with WCAG 2.0, Section 508, or PDF/UA, I am happy to help you achieve your goals.

Services

Consulting

Need an accessibility checkup? I can evaluate your website or mobile application for WCAG 2.0 and Section 508 compliance.

Training

Need a little guidance? I can provided on-site accessibility training for your developers and designers.

Speaking

Need an excellent speaker for a conference or other event? I can speak on a wide variety of accessibility-related topics.
